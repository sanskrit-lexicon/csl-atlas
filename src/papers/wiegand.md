# Wiegand microstructure framing

A reading of MW1899 through Herbert Ernst Wiegand's theory of dictionary microstructure (Wiegand 1989, 2002).

## Central claim

MW exhibits an **integrated microstructure with strong type-bound variation**. The 14 article types each carry a characteristic block profile, with verbal-root articles averaging 9.7 of 18 possible blocks and continuation sub-entries averaging only 4.8. The lexicographer-hedge marker `<ls>L.</ls>` (13.4% of entries) emerges as the most distinctive Wiegandian *commenting structural indicator* — one that PWG (MW's principal source) does not use at all.
